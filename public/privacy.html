Privacy Policy

Introduction

This service operates as a web proxy using Ultraviolet technology. We prioritize user privacy and aim to be transparent about our operations. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

How the Service Works

Our service functions as a client-side proxy, meaning:

  • All page processing happens in your browser
  • Content is fetched through a third-party bare server (not operated by us)
  • Scripts and resources are rewritten in real-time
  • Service workers handle request interception

Third-Party Bare Server

Important: This service relies on an external bare server that we do not own or operate. Please note:

  • The bare server is operated by independent third parties
  • We have no control over the bare server's operations or policies
  • Any data processing at the bare server level is not our responsibility
  • The bare server's availability and performance are beyond our control

Data Collection and Analytics

We collect and process the following types of data:

  • Through the third-party bare server:
    • Requested URLs (for proxying purposes only)
    • Necessary headers to maintain connections
    • Temporary data required for proxy functionality
  • Through analytics services:
    • Google Analytics: Collects anonymous usage data including page views, session duration, and basic device information
    • TelemetryDeck: Gathers anonymous metrics about service performance and feature usage

Important: We do not control how the bare server processes this data. Any data handling at the bare server level is subject to their policies and practices.

Cookies and Tracking

Our service uses the following types of cookies and tracking technologies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for proxy functionality
  • Analytics cookies: Used by Google Analytics and TelemetryDeck to improve our service

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our service.

What We Don't Do

Regarding our frontend service, we want to be clear about what we don't do:

  • We don't store browsing history
  • We don't maintain persistent logs of your proxy usage
  • We don't collect or store personal information
  • We don't sell or share your data with third parties (except for anonymous analytics)

Third-Party Sites

When using our proxy to access other websites:

  • Those sites may have their own privacy policies and tracking methods
  • We can't control what information you provide to those sites
  • We recommend reviewing each site's privacy policy

Technical Details

Our frontend service uses:

  • Service Workers for request interception
  • Client-side JavaScript for content rewriting
  • External bare server for proxy connections (not operated by us)
  • Local storage only for essential proxy functionality

Security

While we implement secure practices on our end, please note:

  • Always use HTTPS when possible
  • Be cautious with sensitive information
  • We can't guarantee the security of third-party sites or the bare server
  • Use this service at your own risk

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use this service or provide any personal information through the service.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to delete your data
  • Right to opt-out of analytics collection
  • Right to lodge a complaint with relevant authorities

Since we collect minimal personal data, most of these rights apply to the analytics data we collect. You can exercise these rights by adjusting your browser settings or using opt-out mechanisms provided by Google Analytics.

DMCA Compliance

We respect intellectual property rights and comply with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA). If you believe that content accessed through our service infringes your copyright, you can submit a DMCA takedown notice. To file a DMCA complaint, please contact us through:

  • Our Discord server (accessible from the main page)

Please include the following in your notice:

  • Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to be infringed
  • Description of where the allegedly infringing material is located
  • Your contact information
  • A statement of good faith belief in the infringement
  • A statement of accuracy under penalty of perjury

Contact Information

For privacy-related inquiries or concerns, please contact us through our Discord server, which can be accessed from the main page.

Changes to Policy
